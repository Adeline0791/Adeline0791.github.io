Tips on Trip

➕ Where can I book hotels in Germany?

If you want to book hotels in your trip, you can download apps like booking, Expedia, Agoda… to find out hotels that you are interested in. Notice that price can be different in different apps and it is better to make a comparison. Sometimes special discounts are randomly provided in different platforms, and you may enjoy a great discount on the hotel that you want to book.

In addition, if you want to book hotels with special design or enjoy local experiences in Germany, please try Airbnb. From flats to even castles, you can find everything you want.

➕ How can I choose the transportation in Germany?

The most recommended way is —— to rent a car! You can find cars rental information in SIXT, hertz, Europcar, Avis… Please check out whether your local driving license is also available in Germany, and do not forget about car insurance. After all preparations, you can enjoy splendid land views in this country!

Besides, trains and buses are also great transportation ways especially for individual passengers. DB is the official railway company in Germany, you can buy tickets in railway stations or in mobile app.

For bus trips, you can also enjoy low price in Germany. Flixbus is a large bus company that operates in Germany, which also has its own apps. GoEuro is also a recommended transportation app, and you can book bus tickets from other small bus companies in this agency.
