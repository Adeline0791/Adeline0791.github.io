Blackforest road

Welcome to the scenic black forest road, whether you are driving a car or riding a motorcycle, you can fully enjoy this journey. If you really come here, you will definitely feel the charming sceneries here. The nearly 70-kilometer route from Feldberg to Waldkirch is like a picture. If the weather is fine, you can have an overview look of the northern Black Forest, enjoy the upper plains of the Rhine to Vogesen (Mountain Ferguson) in Elsass (Alsace, France), and sometimes even enjoy the beautiful scenery of the Austrian Alps.

Schedule you may try:
Day 1: Frankfurt→Heidelberg→Baden-Baden
Day 2: Burg Hohenzollern
Day 3: Fussen→Neuschwanstein
Day 4: Pilgrimage Church of Weiss→Linderhof Palace Day 5: Augsburg→Harburg→nördlingen→Rothenburg
