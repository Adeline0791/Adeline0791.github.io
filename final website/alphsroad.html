Alphs road

Alphs Road -- the natural premier cinema! Welcome to this winding panoramic tour. Starting from Lindau on the Bodensee and ending in Berchtesgaden on the Königssee with a total length of 450 Kilometers, you can run through the magnificent mountain views of southern Bavaria. Go on the road with the appreciation of extreme natural beauty, whether you are driving, cycling or hiking, you can experience the ultimate beauty.

Schedule you may try:
Day 1: Lindau→Fussen→Neuschwanstein
Day 2: Steingaden→Wildsteig→Oberammergau
Day 3: Ettal→Farchant→Garmisch-Partenkirchen
Day 4: Mittenwald→Wallgau→Bad Tölz
Day 5: Landkreis Miesbach→Bernau am Chiemsee→Berchtesgaden
