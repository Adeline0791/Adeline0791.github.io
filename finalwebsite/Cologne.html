Cologne Cathedral

Perhaps nothing is more representative of Cologne rather than Cologne Cathedral, a landmark building in the heart of Cologne. It is a Catholic church, a masterpiece of medieval European Gothic architecture, which is awarded the world's most perfect Gothic church architecture. The twin towers on the west side of the church are one of the peaks of Gothic architecture. The large-scale painted glass windows are layered and decorated with rich triangular urns. The dense and graceful bouquet of bouquets is like a spear that pierces 157 meters. The tall church spires, the semi-circular apse facing the Rhine, and the numerous summits and small towers make up a magnificent spectacle in the history of world architecture.
Built in the mid-13th century, Cologne Cathedral was built to commemorate the saint Ursul. Today, this famous cathedral welcomes 7 million visitors every year.
After the reunification of the two Germans, in order to regain the role of the Capitol, the Capitol building experienced another round of re-construction: adding a special glass dome. Besides, "DEM DEUTSCHEN VOLKE" on the west gate of the Capitol means "for the German people."

Hohenzollernbrücke

The Hohenzollern Bridge is the oldest bridge in Cologne. It was built between 1907 and 1911, consists of three railway bridges and sidewalks. Connected to the cathedral, the Hohenzollern Bridge across the Rhine undoubtedly owns the most beautiful scenery. The Cologne believed that if a lock was placed on the Hohenzollern Bridge and the key was thrown into the Rhine, love could last forever. Therefore, the colorful copper locks on this bridge form a unique landscape.
Address:Hohenzollernbrücke, 50667 Cologne

Rheinauhafen District

Looking out over Cologne Cathedral and Hohenzollern Bridge, the breeze of the Rhine is blowing, the crowds are playing on the riverside, this is a place to forget all your troubles. The Rheinauhafen district becomes a new and unique attraction in Cologne. The avant-garde residential and office buildings are interspersed with many unique cafes, restaurants, art centers and galleries. It is a charming place for people who like art.
